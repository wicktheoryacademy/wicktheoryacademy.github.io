About Wick Theory Academy

Learn trading the right way — clear, practical, and proven.

Our Mission

WickTheory Academy was started to help people learn trading in a simple and practical way. We focus only on strategies that work in live markets. No fake promises, no shortcuts. Just skills that help you trade better.

Our Mentor

Akash has over 7 years of trading experience. Before he even placed his first trade, he spent 4 years only studying and researching the market — learning from different sources, testing ideas, and understanding what really works.

Over time, he combined the best parts of different strategies, removed what didn’t work, and built his own unique approach to trading. Today, Akash teaches Price Action and Smart Money Concepts (SMC) in a way that is clear, direct, and based on real results in the market.

What We Teach

We offer two focused courses:

  • Price Action — reading charts and market movements without relying on indicators.
  • Smart Money Concepts (SMC) — understanding how big players move the market and using it to your advantage.
